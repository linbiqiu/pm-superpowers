# Strategy Business Model

Shape strategic product direction before roadmap or execution.

## Workflow

1. Identify the strategic question and decision owner.
2. Clarify market, customer, and product scope.
3. Gather market, customer, competitor, and business model evidence.
4. Frame strategic options and tradeoffs.
5. Identify assumptions that could invalidate the strategy.
6. Produce a strategy memo or business model recommendation.
7. Red-team the recommendation before roadmap commitment.
