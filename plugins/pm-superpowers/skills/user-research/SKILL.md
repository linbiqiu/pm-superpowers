# User Research

Plan and synthesize research that supports a specific product decision.

## Required References

Read `../../references/chinese-output-standard.md` first. Read `../../references/evidence-taxonomy.md`, `../../references/gate-system.md`, and `../../references/supporting-pm-skills.md`.

## Supporting PM Skills

Use available method skills when useful: `interview-script`, `summarize-interview`, `user-personas`, `customer-journey-map`, `identify-assumptions-new`, and `prioritize-assumptions`.

## Workflow

1. Define the decision research should inform.
2. Identify target participants and sampling gaps.
3. Draft research questions and interview script.
4. Classify findings into quotes, signals, assumptions, and opportunities.
5. Produce insights and implications.
6. Recommend next workflow: discovery, PRD, roadmap, or experiment.

## Block Conditions

Block when research has no decision purpose. Do not conduct broad interviews without knowing what product choice the research will affect.
