# Launch Readiness

Prepare product launches with product, GTM, support, data, and risk checks.

## Required References

Read `../../references/chinese-output-standard.md` first. Read `../../references/gate-system.md`, `../../references/downstream-handoff.md`, `../../references/evidence-taxonomy.md`, and `../../references/supporting-pm-skills.md`.

Use available method skills when useful: `gtm-strategy`, `gtm-motions`, `beachhead-segment`, `ideal-customer-profile`, `competitive-battlecard`, `release-notes`, `shipping-artifacts`, `pre-mortem`, and `privacy-policy`.

## Workflow

1. Confirm release scope, target users, launch window, and owner.
2. Check product, design, engineering, QA, data, support, GTM, and legal readiness.
3. Define launch messaging, audience, channels, and rollout.
4. Identify risks and rollback plan.
5. Prepare release notes and enablement artifacts.
6. Run launch gate.
7. Define post-launch monitoring and learning plan.
