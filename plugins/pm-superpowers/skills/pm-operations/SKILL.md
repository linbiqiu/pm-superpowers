# PM Operations

Run consistent product operating rhythms.

## Required References

Read `../../references/chinese-output-standard.md` first. Read `../../references/evidence-taxonomy.md`, `../../references/gate-system.md`, and `../../references/supporting-pm-skills.md`.

## Supporting PM Skills

Use available method skills when useful: `summarize-meeting`, `sprint-plan`, `retro`, `stakeholder-map`, `brainstorm-okrs`, `strategy-red-team`, and `release-notes`.

## Workflow

1. Identify the operating context: meeting, sprint, retro, OKR, stakeholder alignment, or follow-up.
2. Extract decisions, commitments, risks, and open questions.
3. Separate facts from opinions and assumptions.
4. Produce the operational artifact.
5. Assign owners and next dates.
6. Route unresolved product questions to the right scenario workflow.

Use:

## Block Conditions

Block only when the requested operational output depends on unknown decisions or owners. Otherwise produce a concise artifact and mark open items.
