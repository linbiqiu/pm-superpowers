# New Product Discovery

Guide early product work from idea to validated direction.

## Required References

Read `../../references/chinese-output-standard.md` first. Read `../../references/scene-taxonomy.md`, `../../references/evidence-taxonomy.md`, `../../references/gate-system.md`, and `../../references/supporting-pm-skills.md`.

## Supporting PM Skills

Use available method skills when useful: `user-segmentation`, `user-personas`, `job-stories`, `opportunity-solution-tree`, `identify-assumptions-new`, `prioritize-assumptions`, `brainstorm-experiments-new`, `value-proposition`, and `lean-canvas`.

## Workflow

1. Clarify target user, problem, business intent, and decision needed.
2. Classify current evidence and assumptions.
3. Define the opportunity and desired outcome.
4. Map solution options only after the problem is clear.
5. Identify highest-risk assumptions.
6. Propose validation experiments.
7. Run a problem and opportunity gate before recommending PRD or design work.

## Block Conditions

Block when target user, problem, or decision is unknown. Do not produce a PRD until the problem gate passes or the user explicitly accepts the risks.
